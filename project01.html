Digital Literacy

For my Digital Literacy Project, a lot of my work was experimental.
Glitch, Video, and Sound art were things I hadn't done before
but I tried to keep the theme of replaying memories. For the
final website, I based it on my oldest memory of the internet
being customizing social media pages so I recreated Kim
Kardashians MySpace Page.
